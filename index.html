Get to Know Us

International Miracle Church, formerly known as 'Miracle Tamil Assembly of God', was started on December 23rd 1990 with 12 believers. The founding pastor was Rev. Dr. Thomas Gnanasekar; he continues to faithfully serve the Church as its senior pastor to date.

In spite of starting out small, the Church grew rapidly over the years. In 1993, International Miracle Church saw the largest growth it had ever seen in its history; in fact it was one of the fastest growths the nation of Canada had ever seen. Between the years 1990 and 1995, over 800 new believers were baptized and added to the Body of Christ. International Miracle church thus opened up a new chapter in the history of the Tamil speaking church in Canada. Several other churches were born out of this ministry and the word of God spread across this vast community.

Today with about 200 believers, International Miracle Church is a growing and dynamic church working with its sole vision of reaching the lost and touching lives with the love of God.

Located at 585 Middlefield Rd., Unit #33, in Scarborough, ON, International Miracle Church is marching on in these end times, fulfilling the great commission Jesus gave us: “therefore go and make disciples of all nations.” (Mathew 28:19)

Missions in Sri Lanka

ʻNew Beginningsʼ is the compassionate arm of International Miracle Mission Inc. (A Registered Canadian Charity - Registration No. 874660426) that currently operates in Sri Lanka.

Sri Lanka is a beautiful island nation in the Indian ocean that has been ravaged by over three decades of gruesome civil war. Today, the war has ended and things seem to have returned to normal. There are no actual sights or sounds of war, but the cruel effects of the war still linger on in many parts of the island.

Especially in the Mullivaikal community that we work in, we have come across so many tragic stories, so many broken lives, and so much heartache everywhere. The things we have witnessed here is heart breaking. Far more debilitating than the loss of material possessions or lives is the loss of a person's self-worth; the self-esteem and morale of people has been crushed. Those who survived the war are in a seemingly worse shape than those who perished. They are alive, yet without a purpose in life; they awake each morning with the sunrise, but are still imprisoned in the dark night of their futile lives.

Our vision is to ultimately see the Mullivaikal community restored through the work done by ʻNew Beginnings'. In order to see this transformation take place, we believe that peoples’ lives need to be rebuilt first. We want to see people revitalized and given a sense of purpose and direction. We invite you to learn more about our work and play a part in this rebuilding process.

Miracle Academy of Biblical Studies

Miracle Academy of Biblical Studies was established in September 2005 as the fruition of a God given vision to our founder, Rev. Dr. Thomas Gnanasekar. Throughout his nearly 45 years of ministry, the Lord has used Rev. Gnanasekar to train up and equip several hundreds of pastors and evangelists and lay workers who serve in God’s Kingdom all over the world.

Although he has always been training leaders and workers in the local Church context, it has been Rev. Gnanasekar’s passion to see an accredited Bible College established. The Lord gave Rev. Gnanasekar this vision in 1975 while he was in prayer on a mountaintop in Chennai, India. 30 years later, his vision came to pass with the help of Rev. Dr. John McDonald who was the director of Proximity Institutes at Canada Christian College at the time.

MABS, although initially started in Toronto, Ontario, has now expanded into Montreal, New Jersey, UK, Quatar, India and Sri Lanka. Over 650 students have graduated from MABS and serve the Lord in various capacities their local churches.
